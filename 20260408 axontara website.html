Click2BIM — How it works

From scanner
to model in five steps.

A fully automated, AI-driven pipeline that turns raw LiDAR data into BIM-ready deliverables — without manual tracing.

01
Scan & Upload

Capture your building with a Leica, Faro, or compatible scanner. Upload the .e57, .las, or .laz file directly to the platform.

Any scanner
02
SLIM Pre-process

Click2SLIM automatically compresses and optimises your point cloud — reducing file size up to 95% while preserving geometric fidelity.

Automatic
03
AI Segmentation

Deep learning models classify every point: walls, floors, ceilings, structural elements, openings, MEP features — all labelled semantically.

AI-powered
04
Parametric Extraction

Classified geometry is converted into fully parametric BIM objects — dimensions, materials, openings, and relationships built in.

LOD 300+
05
QA & Download

Deviation maps, clash reports, and export in IFC, DXF, DWG, RVT, or PLN. Your model is ready for design, FM, or coordination.

All formats
